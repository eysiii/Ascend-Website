OUR COMPANY

ASCEND PLUS ELEVATOR & ESCALATOR CORPORATION is a corporation duly organized by elevator professionals from Philippines-Taiwan-Singapore on May 2015 to offer the best quality products and services for all elevator and escalator needs.

We are engaged in elevator and escalator industries for almost 16 years, with leading manufacturers and suppliers of elevator and escalator equipment.

It is our commitment to insist for our employees in pursuing excellence and in leading the industry; to innovate our product and technology; listening to our customers and providing the most professional service that they deserve; quality delivery of services and goods. It is of utmost importance to give the best product and the best service that we can give.

MISSION

To be the one of the leading supplier of elevator and escalator equipment providing well-trained personnel and committing to equality and round the clock response and support to our client.

VISION

A company that is at par with the leading elevator supplier in the country, building up relationships with customers that would last, recognized for its competence and quick action in tune to the needs of the customers.

COMPETENCE - employing dependable and consistent technicians and personnel to give the satisfaction that our clients deserve.
